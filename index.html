Abstract

How can autonomous robots and vehicles predict pedestrian behavior with both high accuracy and social intelligence? This workshop at ICRA 2026 tackles the core challenges in prediction that impact pedestrian safety and interaction fluency. We move beyond technical fundamentals to explore cutting-edge solutions at the intersection of machine learning, AI, kinematics, and cognitive psychology.

The event will feature:

  1. Keynote talks from leading researchers illuminating the path forward;
  2. Oral and poster presentations showcasing the latest breakthroughs from submitted papers;
  3. Dynamic discussions and networking sessions to connect with academia and industry peers.

Join us to shape the future of interactive autonomy. Share your insights, discover collaborative opportunities, and help bridge the gap between prediction and safe, natural human-robot coexistence.
